https://www.cms.gov/OpenPayments.  This dataset makes financial relationships between physicians and medical companies more transparent. You can extract the total dollar value of payments made to individual physicians.

https://open.fda.gov/tools/downloads/.  The FDA has posted many datasets here, including food recalls, adverse reactions to drugs, and approved medical devices.

https://healthdata.gov. This site makes health data more accessible to entrepreneurs, researchers, and policy makers, with the hopes of increasingly better health outcomes for all.
